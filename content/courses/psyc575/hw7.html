Learning Objective

  • Independently analyze real data using statistical software for multilevel modeling

Assignment Description

In this assignment, you will use the data from the brief report by Raykos et al. (2018), which included 134 patients receiving an enhanced cognitive–behavioral therapy from public eating disorders service. All patients met the DSM-5 criteria for anorexia nervosa (AN). You will fit growth models on the eating disorder symptoms (on a range of 0 to 6) over five weekly intervals throughout treatment. Then, you will choose from a list of covariates to predict differences in the symptom trajectories.
